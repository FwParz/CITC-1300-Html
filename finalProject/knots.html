Knots to Know

Figure 8

Disclaimer if you tie these wrong and die, it is because of your own incompetence. Now that that's out of the way This video I stole from REIs youtube demonstrates how to tie a perfect figure 8 knot. This is the standard knot that almost all (99%) climbers use. The best part about this knot is that even if you tie it drastically wrong, if it's through the correct hoops on your gear, you'll most likely be perfectly fine. If you are still scared because of my first statement, good, but you should watch Hard Is Easy try his hardest to produce rope breaking drops.
