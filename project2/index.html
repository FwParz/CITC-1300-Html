Welcome! Prepare for the basics of how to climb!

To get started the very first thing you need is some shoes. Climbing shoes are made to be pointy, stiff, and grippy. It's enitely possible to climb without special shoes, or even barefoot, but climbing shoes are built to make your climbing easier and less painful.

Now if your commited you can get out there and find a climbing gym to climb in! There are a few more things that will make your experience more enjoyable though. You can find them in the Gear Tab!
